Getting to know the simulator

Hi, I am Bucky, a robot who is tasked with helping humans in any way possible. I am just a small child right now and need to be trained. Instructions are given to me by humans and I have to complete them efficiently. I can make use of all objects around me. I want to minimise the effort and time taken to complete the action. Can you help me with some of the instructions that humans have given me?

Components of the simulator

  1. This is Bucky, the robot you will be controlling.
  2. ZOOM IN
  3. ZOOM OUT
  4. Rotate the view of the robot left
  5. Rotate the view of the robot right
  6. Undo Move: reversing a move made wrongly.
